Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024
Privacy First: noise.garden is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data, store it securely, and delete it automatically through our ephemeral system.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen display name (3-20 characters)
  • Password: Securely hashed and encrypted
  • Avatar: Optional profile picture (if provided)
  • Bio: Optional profile description (if provided)
  • Backup Codes: For account recovery purposes

1.2 Content Data

  • Posts: Text content you publish (max 280 characters)
  • Images: Photos you upload with posts
  • Replies: Responses to other users' posts
  • Polls: Poll questions and options you create
  • Mentions: When you tag other users

1.3 Technical Information

  • IP Address: For security and rate limiting
  • User Agent: Browser and device information
  • Session Data: Authentication tokens (temporary)
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized platform usage statistics

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real names or personal identification
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Browsing history outside our platform
  • Third-party social media profiles
  • Financial or payment information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Platform Functions

  • Display your posts and profile to other users
  • Enable user interactions (replies, mentions)
  • Authenticate your account access
  • Facilitate the invitation system

2.2 Safety and Security

  • Prevent spam and abuse
  • Enforce community guidelines
  • Rate limit requests to prevent system abuse
  • Investigate reported violations

2.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Improve platform performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Monitor system health and stability

3. Data Retention and Deletion

3.1 Automatic Expiration

Ephemeral by Design: All content on noise.garden automatically expires and is permanently deleted.
Data Type Default Retention User Control
Posts 30 days Customizable (1 hour - 30 days)
Replies 30 days Customizable (1 hour - 30 days)
Images 30 days Expires with associated post
Polls 30 days Customizable (1 hour - 30 days)
Account Data Until account deletion Delete anytime
Login Sessions 30 days Logout anytime

3.2 Account Deletion

  • Users can delete their accounts at any time
  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • All associated data is immediately removed
  • Deleted usernames become available for reuse after 90 days

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Sales: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4.1 When We Share Data

  • Public Posts: Visible to all platform users (by design)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Safety Concerns: To prevent harm or illegal activity
  • Platform Security: To protect against technical attacks

4.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers for:

  • Database Hosting: Neon (PostgreSQL hosting)
  • CDN Services: For fast content delivery
  • Security Services: DDoS protection and monitoring

All service providers are bound by strict data protection agreements.

5. Security Measures

5.1 Technical Security

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Password Security: Hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Rate Limiting: Prevents brute force attacks
  • Input Validation: Protects against injection attacks
  • Security Headers: XSS protection, CSP, and more

5.2 Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication for administrator accounts
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Minimal staff access to user data
  • Secure development practices and code reviews

5.3 Incident Response

  • 24/7 monitoring for security threats
  • Automated alerting for suspicious activity
  • Rapid response team for security incidents
  • User notification for any data breaches (within 72 hours)

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all data associated with your account
  • Modify Information: Update profile, bio, and settings
  • Download Data: Export your content before it expires
  • Delete Account: Permanently remove all data

6.2 Privacy Settings

  • Control who can mention you
  • Hide your online status
  • Make posts unlisted by default
  • Hide profile from search engines

6.3 Communication Preferences

  • Disable push notifications
  • Control mention notifications
  • Set quiet hours for notifications
  • Mute specific words or topics

7. International Users

7.1 Data Transfers

  • Data is primarily stored in secure data centers
  • Transfers comply with applicable privacy laws
  • Appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers

7.2 Legal Compliance

  • GDPR (EU): Full compliance with European privacy rights
  • CCPA (California): Compliance with California privacy laws
  • PIPEDA (Canada): Adherence to Canadian privacy standards
  • Other Jurisdictions: Compliance with applicable local laws

8. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: This platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • Users must confirm they are at least 13 years old
  • Users under 18 should have parental consent
  • Any accounts discovered to belong to children under 13 will be deleted
  • Parents can contact us to request deletion of a child's account

9. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy periodically
  • Material changes will be communicated 30 days in advance
  • Users will be notified through platform announcements
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Previous versions are archived for reference
